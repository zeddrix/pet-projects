GAME RULES

Clues

You can only get 3 clues for each level. After you've used all 3, you would be answering the next questions without any clue, of course. So use it wisely.

Time Limit

You can enable and disable this option on the Settings. When enabled, you have exactly 10 seconds to answer each question. Once the time is up without you answering the current question, it will proceed to the next question. Consequently, you won't earn a point for this.

ABOUT

About Bible query

This is just a simple Bible game that we can play with our friends and families. If you're one of Jehovah's Witnesses, you can play this game on your next family worship!

The Bible verses that served as clues in this game are all from the New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures. While the other clues are from some Bible-based publications also published by The Watch Tower Bible and Tract Society of Pennsylvania. For example, some of these clues are from the book Insight on the Scriptures.

These Bible-based publications can be downloaded for free on Jehovah’s Witnesses' official website on: www.jw.org/en.

Note that the sources of the clues used are not abbreviated. For example, instead of having "it-2 853-854", this game used "Insight on the Scriptures, Volume 2 pp. 853-854," for the sake of our non-Witness players. Note, though, that the abbreviation "nwt" is an exception. It just means New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures.

To know the rules of the game, please go to Rules.
